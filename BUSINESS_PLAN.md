# CMU Talent Hub - Business Plan

## Business Model, Product/Service, and Target Market

**Product/Service:** CMU Talent Hub is a verified, secure platform connecting Carnegie Mellon University students with opportunities, peers, and businesses. We combine the networking power of LinkedIn with the talent marketplace model of Upwork, built exclusively for the CMU community.

**Business Model:**
- **Freemium Model:** Free basic profiles for all CMU students
- **Premium Subscriptions:** Enhanced features for students ($9.99/month) and businesses ($49.99/month)
- **Transaction Fees:** 5% commission on successful job placements and project collaborations
- **Featured Listings:** Businesses pay $99/month for premium job postings with enhanced visibility
- **Campus Partnerships:** Revenue sharing agreements with CMU career services and departments

**Target Market:**
- **Primary:** 15,000+ CMU students seeking internships, projects, and career opportunities
- **Secondary:** 1,400+ CMU faculty members looking for student collaborators and research assistants
- **Tertiary:** Hundreds of businesses actively recruiting from CMU (tech companies, startups, consulting firms, nonprofits)

---

## Technology Integration

**Platform Architecture:**
- **Frontend:** Next.js 15 + React 19 web application with responsive design
- **Mobile:** React Native + Expo cross-platform mobile app (iOS & Android)
- **Backend:** NestJS RESTful API with PostgreSQL database
- **Real-time Features:** WebSocket integration for instant messaging and notifications
- **AI/ML Integration:** 
  - Intelligent talent matching algorithm based on skills, projects, and preferences
  - Automated job recommendation engine
  - Smart search with natural language processing
- **Security:** JWT authentication, role-based access control, encrypted communications, CMU email verification
- **File Management:** Cloud storage integration for portfolio media (videos, images, code repositories)
- **Analytics:** Data-driven insights for students (profile views, engagement metrics) and businesses (candidate pipeline analytics)

**Technology Advantages:**
- Scalable microservices architecture ready for multi-university expansion
- API-first design enabling future integrations with university systems
- Mobile-first approach ensuring accessibility across all devices
- Automated verification system reducing manual administrative overhead
