# Growth Goals & Community Impact

## Growth Goals for Next 12-24 Months

### Launch Strategy:
We launch at CMU — 15,000+ students, 1,400 faculty, and active recruiters — then scale nationally.

### Growth Trajectory:
- **Year 1:** 5,000 users at CMU (validation phase)
- **Year 2:** 20,000 users across 3 universities
- **Year 3:** 50,000+ users across 10+ universities
- **Long-term:** 500,000+ verified students and global reach in a $200B talent market

- **User Acquisition:** 5,000+ registered CMU students (validation phase)
- **Active Users:** 1,000+ monthly active users
- **Business Partners:** 50+ businesses actively posting opportunities
- **Faculty Engagement:** 100+ faculty members using the platform
- **Job Placements:** 200+ successful job placements and collaborations
- **Revenue:** $150,000+ annual revenue
- **Platform Metrics:** 4.5+ star app rating, 60%+ user retention rate
- **Team Growth:** 8-10 full-time employees

### 24-Month Goals (Year 2):
- **Multi-University Expansion:** Launch at 3 additional top-tier universities
- **Total Users:** 20,000+ registered users across all campuses
- **Active Users:** 4,000+ monthly active users
- **Business Network:** 200+ businesses across all campuses
- **Job Placements:** 1,000+ successful placements annually
- **Revenue:** $855,000+ annual revenue
- **Team Growth:** 20-25 full-time employees

### Year 3 Goals:
- **Total Users:** 50,000+ registered users across 10+ universities
- **Active Users:** 15,000+ monthly active users
- **Business Network:** 500+ businesses across all campuses
- **Job Placements:** 3,000+ successful placements annually
- **Revenue:** $2.5M+ annual revenue
