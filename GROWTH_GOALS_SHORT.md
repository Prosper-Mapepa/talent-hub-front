# Growth Goals & Community Impact

## Growth Goals: 12-24 Months

**Launch Strategy:**
We launch at CMU — 15,000+ students, 1,400 faculty, and active recruiters — then scale nationally.

**Growth Trajectory:**
- **Year 1:** 5,000 users at CMU (validation phase)
- **Year 2:** 20,000 users across 3 universities
- **Year 3:** 50,000+ users across 10+ universities
- **Long-term:** 500,000+ verified students and global reach in a $200B talent market

**12-Month Milestones:**
- 5,000 registered users at CMU
- 1,000+ monthly active users
- 50+ business partners
- 200+ successful job placements
- $150K+ revenue
- 8-10 direct jobs created

**24-Month Milestones:**
- 20,000 users across 3 universities
- 200+ business partners
- 1,000+ job placements annually
- $855K+ revenue
- 20-25 direct jobs created

---

## Jobs Created at Full Scale

**Direct Employment:** 40-50 jobs by Year 3, growing to 100-150+ at full scale (software engineers, product managers, sales, marketing, operations, customer success)

**Indirect Job Creation:** 3,000+ student job placements by Year 3, growing to 10,000+ annually at full scale (internships, part-time, full-time positions)

---

## Contribution to Eastern Kentucky Economy & Community

**Economic Impact:**
- **Direct Jobs:** 40-50 high-paying tech jobs by Year 3 ($2.6M+ in annual wages)
- **Student Earnings:** Facilitate $5M+ in student earnings through job placements by Year 3
- **Tax Revenue:** $200K+ in state and local taxes by Year 3
- **Business Support:** Connect regional businesses with top university talent, reducing hiring costs

**Community Impact:**
- **Talent Retention:** Help retain top talent in Eastern Kentucky by connecting students with local opportunities
- **Rural Access:** Use technology to connect rural students with opportunities regardless of location
- **Workforce Development:** Build sustainable talent pipeline for regional employers
- **Economic Diversification:** Support emerging industries by matching students with innovative businesses
- **University Partnerships:** Partner with regional universities to create comprehensive talent networks

**Long-term:** Position Eastern Kentucky as an innovation hub, attract businesses through talent access, and contribute to sustainable regional economic growth.
